Solutions

Custom Web Design

Does your business or organization need an internet presence or a refreshed look and feel? Let us provide a vivid web solution to you! We offer a fully customizable website solution, using the latest technology, that can bring your vision to reality. If you do not have a clear vision for your website, no worries, we can help inspire and guide you through that process as well. Not only will your website look great, the functionalty will be intuitive and work as expected.

All of our designs are based on a responsive framework, which allows your site to adapt according to the device used to access it. This framework will ensure your site always displays just the way you intended it to. The design will be responsive to mobile devices (e.g. cellphones and tablets), which will allow users to easily navigate the site on all of their devices.

We adhere to the latest OWASP standards, so if your website requires the storage and access of sensitive data, rest assured that your data will be stored and accessed securely.

Research Automation (Data Mining)

Do you find yourself or your employees spending a vast amount of time researching work related subjects on the internet? ViViD can replace this time consuming, repetitive task with an automated solution.

Data mining techniques can be used to extract information from websites that you already use. We place the extracted information into a database that can be easily navigated via a custom desktop application. Research that ordinarily might take months or years to complete can be done in minutes.

All you would have to provide to us is a list of keywords and the website address(es) that would normally be used to do your manual research and we can do it all for you!
